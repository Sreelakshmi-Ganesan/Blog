The Powernap Paradox: When 10 Minutes Becomes 2 Hours

Powernaps are supposed to be quick, right? Well, mine have a tendency to turn into full-blown snooze sessions.

My Struggle with Powernaps

Published on Oct 10, 2024

It all starts with a simple goal: a quick 10-minute nap. But somewhere along the way, time slips away, and I wake up feeling like I've entered a different time zone:

The 10-Minute Promise

I tell myself, "Just 10 minutes," but before I know it, an hour has passed, and I'm in a deep slumber. How did that happen?

The 2-Hour Nap

Eventually, I gave up on the whole "short nap" idea. Now, I embrace the 2-hour nap, and while it’s great, it leaves me groggy and disoriented when I wake up. But at least I feel rested!

Lessons Learned

Despite my battles with the clock, I’ve learned to accept that some naps are just meant to be longer than planned. Maybe next time, I'll just set an alarm.

Conclusion

If you’ve ever found yourself in the powernap paradox, you know the struggle is real. But who can resist the sweet temptation of an extra hour of sleep?
